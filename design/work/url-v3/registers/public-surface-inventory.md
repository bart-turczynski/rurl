## Purpose

Durable G1 inventory (not a memory summary) of the entire rurl public surface at
the frozen baseline **89be90b**: every exported function, its arguments and
defaults, its behavior dials, cross-argument/cross-function interactions, its
vector/error behavior, its documentation location, its migration surface, and
its exercising test family — plus the 18 public parsed-output fields and the
curl dependency + migration surface flagged by reconciliation §10. Each item
carries an inventory disposition with a `file:line` evidence locator so the
claim is independently checkable, and any missing/inaccessible evidence is
recorded explicitly (never silently omitted), per §7 G1.

`state` is `DISCOVERED` for every row: frozen source identity, baseline
(89be90b), and evidence location are recorded; onward transitions (this register
`PROPOSED` → … → normatively closed at gate **G3** against §6 artifacts 3 and
5–10, per §7 G1/G3 and RCON-05) belong to later gates. This register is the
inventory phase only; it settles no open v3 product decision.

## Scope and evidence conventions

- **Baseline byte-identity.** The orchestrator verified `NAMESPACE`, `R/`,
  `man/`, `DESCRIPTION`, and `tests/` are byte-identical between 89be90b and the
  working tree; locators cite working-tree `file:line` directly.
- **29-export bijection.** `NAMESPACE` has exactly 29 `export(...)` lines; the
  `exported-function`/`exported-data` rows are exactly that set — no missing, no
  extra (mechanically enforced, mirroring G1.1's row-count bijection). At the
  baseline all 29 exports are functions; there are zero exported data objects.
- **18 public fields vs 20 Stage A fields (§10).** The public parsed-output
  projection (`R/utils.R` `.spu_result_fields`, 18 fields) is narrower than the
  internal Stage A template (`.spu_stage_a_fields`, 20 fields). The inventory
  does NOT stop at parse functions: all 18 public fields are `public-output-field`
  rows. The 20 Stage A fields are internal state, out of the public surface, and
  are named here as explicit non-omitted context, not as public rows.
- **curl + migration surface (§10).** `curl` removal "needs more than replacing
  the parser call" — metadata import plus parsing, path/query, accessor, and
  email uses. These are inventoried as explicit `curl-dependency` rows plus a
  `migration-surface` disposition row; per-item migration is also a column.
- **Defaults.** For `match.arg` arguments the listed default is the effective
  first choice; the full choice set is recorded under behavior_dials.
